I am currently a PhD candidate at INRIA and École Polytechnique, under the supervison of Catuscia Palamidessi.

I received my BSc and MSc degrees in Computer Science from the Department of Informatics and Telecommunications at the University of Athens. My Master thesis was Tor: Tree-based Vanguards under the supervision of Konstantinos Chatzikokolakis.

My work is funded by the project CRYPTECS: Lifting Privacy-Preserving Computing Technologies into the Cloud, a Franco/German ANR/BMBF-supported project.

My research focuses on:

  • Differential Privacy
  • Location Privacy (d-privacy)
  • Privacy-Preserving Federated Learning
  • Quantitative Information Flow

  • 2023-2024: Computer Programing CSE102, École Polytechnique
  • 2021-2023: Computer Security, National and Kapodistrian University of Athens
  • 2020-2021: Introduction to Programming, National and Kapodistrian University of Athens
